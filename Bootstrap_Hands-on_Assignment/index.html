About

The Geopolitics Online is a service mainly focusing on providing a blog discussing geopolitical issues and a regularly updated database of useful sources for curious learners of geopolitical matters.

The Geopolitics Online's website, the blog, and the sources page are especially meant for the students of international relations, political science, and geopolitics, but also for anyone interested in developing their knowledge in the field of geopolitics.
